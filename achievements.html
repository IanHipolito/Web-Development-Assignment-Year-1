Club Achievements

It is not unknown that Paris Saint Germain is a very successful football team when it comes to winning trophies. Paris Saint Germain Football Club holds many records, most notably being the most successful French club in history in terms of official titles won, with 45. Additionally, PSG have won 25 unofficial titles.

Paris Saint Germain have won all four national titles in a single season on four occasions. This feat is known as the domestic quadruple. The Red and Blues have completed the domestic double, the league and league cup double, the domestic cup double and the domestic treble several times as well. Therefore, PSG are the club with the most domestic doubles and league and league cup doubles, and the only team to have won the domestic cup double, the domestic treble and the domestic quadruple.
