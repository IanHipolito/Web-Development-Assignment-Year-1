PSG 2021/2022 KITS

PSG KITS

The Paris Saint Germain kits have come a very long way from what they used to be. Their kits are no longer just for playing football, but also now very much street wear and fashion. With Paris Saint Germain's evolution of their kits throughout the years, their jerseys have become more stylish and fashionable. With the collaboration with Jordan since September 2018, PSG's kits have sky rocketed in popularity. More and more people not even football fans are wearing PSG's jersey now a days. With their new release of their 2021/2022, I would like you to scroll and take a look at thei new and improved kits.

PSG HOME KIT

PSG HOME KIT

Instead of the tradional navy with a red stripe and two white stripes along the red one, Paris Saint Germain and Jordan have switched it up this year and went for a more simplistic all navy jersey with the tradional colours on the collar and sleeves of the jersey. This gives it a nice clean finish and especially with the Jordan logo it is quite stylish. They have also went for a more basketball type pair of football shorts which adds a bit of individuality to the kit as no other kit is like it.

PSG AWAY KIT

PSG AWAY KIT

Paris Saint Germain's away kit this year is absolutly stunning, the use of pink and white and the way the two colours contrast eachother adds to the jersey. The two colours match eachother well along with the black stripes.

PSG THIRD KIT

PSG THIRD KIT

The third kit is a more darker kit as it is black and grey. Instead of the PSG crest on the left hand side of the jersey it is in the middle. Not a lot can be said about this kit other than it has a very nice and sleek look to it.

PSG FOURTH KIT

PSG FOURTH KIT

The fourth kit has to be one of the most unique kits Paris Saint Germain has brought out in a while. It is pink and purple with a pattern spray look to it. If you like to be flashy this kit is for you as you will definitly get the attention of everyone you walk past. It is a very trendy jersey and many people will buy this jersey due to its unique design and colour way. If Paris Saint Germain continue to bring out jerseys like these ones, they will definitly at the top of the trend some day.
